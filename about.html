About Us

Prime Scholars School

Prime Scholars’ School is a coeducational school in Abuja, Nigeria serving grades from Pre-School through Senior Secondary. The school has campuses located at Karu and Kubwa, with State of Standard facilities to ensure convenient and inclusive learning for all students Prime Scholars School is a school perfectly Situated in serene and secure areas in parts of Abuja. The Security for our students and the provision of a serene environment to ensure convenient learning for our students while not compromising on the quality of Education our students get to enjoy is our top priority. Come aboard and join us in training leaders of their generation.

Mission Statement

To provide exceptional care to our pupils and students; while fostering each child’s intellectual, social, physical, and moral development, in a rich academic environment

Our Focus

We love nurturing children to and through maturity, Celebrating over a decade of guiding students to educational success

Our Vision

To provide a range of world-class educational and social experiences, with an environment appropriate to the age, potential, and needs of our pupils and students, in order to empower them with the knowledge needed to be self-reliant in a competitive world
